What is Rainy Day Club?

Rainy Day Club is a magical world where kids can play with creative AI agents. Draw, laugh, discover, and imagine, especially when the weather turns gray.

Rainy Day Club is a kid-friendly platform with AI activities for children at home and in class. Create a custom AI coloring page, invent a fun challenge, generate a joke, or write a song together. Parents, teachers, and kids quickly get creative inspiration that matches age, topic, and energy.
