How does it work?

In three simple steps, children are immediately creative.

1. Choose an agent

Pick who helps today: coloring page creator, challenge generator, character builder or songwriter.

2. Create together

Kids enter an idea and instantly see it come to life.

3. Share and keep creating

Save the creation, print it out or make another version right away.

What can kids create?

Each tool feels like a mini-adventure with its own style.

Custom coloring pages

Challenges

Character builder

Songs

Let kids make their own song around their theme or favorite topic.
